Senior Software Engineer - Full Stack

Company: TechSolutions Inc.
Location: Remote (with occasional travel to New York HQ)
Type: Full-time

About Us:
TechSolutions Inc. is a fast-growing technology company specializing in enterprise SaaS solutions. We build scalable, user-friendly applications that help businesses streamline their operations and improve productivity. Our flagship product serves over 5,000 companies worldwide.

Job Description:
We are seeking an experienced Senior Software Engineer to join our engineering team. The ideal candidate will design, develop, and maintain high-quality software for our enterprise applications. You will work closely with product managers, designers, and other engineers to build robust features and services.

Responsibilities:
- Design, develop, and maintain scalable and efficient code for our web applications
- Contribute to full-stack development including frontend, backend, and database design
- Collaborate with cross-functional teams to define, design, and ship new features
- Participate in code reviews and mentor junior developers
- Implement automated testing strategies and improve code quality
- Troubleshoot and resolve complex technical issues in production environments
- Work with DevOps to improve CI/CD pipelines and infrastructure
- Contribute to technical documentation and architectural decisions
- Stay updated with latest industry trends and technologies

Requirements:
- Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or related field
- 5+ years of professional software development experience
- Strong proficiency in JavaScript/TypeScript and at least one backend language (Python, Java, Go)
- Experience with modern web frameworks (React, Angular, or Vue.js)
- Knowledge of database systems (SQL and NoSQL)
- Familiarity with cloud platforms (AWS, GCP, or Azure)
- Experience with version control systems (Git) and CI/CD pipelines
- Strong problem-solving skills and attention to detail
- Excellent communication and collaboration abilities
- Experience with agile development methodologies

Preferred Qualifications:
- Experience with microservices architecture
- Knowledge of containerization (Docker, Kubernetes)
- Experience with serverless technologies
- Understanding of security best practices
- Contributions to open-source projects
- Experience with monitoring and performance optimization
